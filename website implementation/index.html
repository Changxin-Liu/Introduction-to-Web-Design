About The Martian

Author

Andrew Taylor Weir (born June 16, 1972) is an American novelist whose debut novel, The Martian, was later adapted into a film of the same name directed by Ridley Scott in 2015. He also worked as a computer programmer for much of his life.

The Martian

Overview

When a dust storm forces his crew to evacuate the planet while thinking him dead, astronaut Mark Watney finds hismself stranded on Mars's surface, completely alone.

Armed with nothing but his ingenuity, his engineering skills-and a gallows sense of humor that proves to be his greatest source of strength-Mark embarks on a dogged quest to stay alive. But will his resourcefulness be enough to overcome the impossible odds against him?
